The Best Movies According to Mary Lou

My top three movies of all time

    The Fiddler On the Roof

    I loved the music and the dancing in this movie. I also loved learning about the Jewish traditions and the family connections. Tevye was a great narrator and he loves his family. He has to deal with the changing times. But, oh so sad in the end.

    Interview with a Vampire

    I'm a big Anne Rice fan. I find this romantic fantasy about immortality at a price to be intriguing. I especially like the New Orleans background.

    There's Something About Mary

    This movie has many memorable moments. I laughed so many times the first time I saw it. There are scenes that I'll always remember.
